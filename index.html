Welcome Enthusiasts and History Buffs!

Learn about the rich history of the motocicletta.

Italians have always been a leader in art and design. A name that first comes to mind that emphasizes this is Leonardo Da Vinci. During his time, he was technologically ahead of the rest of the world. From the 19th century to today, Italy has also been a leader in the automotive and motorcycle industry. Companies like Alfa Romeo, Ferrari, Ducati, and Vespa are a few examples of this. The beautiful designs of Italian four and two-wheeled vehicles are a testament to the craftsmanship they can achieve.

If you would like to learn more about Italian motorcycles or their manufacturers, you have come to the right place. Here you will find the history, as well as some more recent events, surrounding the likes of Aprila, Moto Guzzi, MV Agusta, Vespa, and more.
