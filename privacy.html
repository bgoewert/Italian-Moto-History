About Us

This site was originally built by Brennan Goewert as a final project for IS 139 "Web Publishing" at St. Louis Community College. Project wise, this site incorporates several different elements that fulfil requirements for the final. The main purpose of this site is to display, and hopefully educate visitors on, the rich history of Italian motorcycles as well as provide a directory for events and museum locations where visitors can find where to experience these Italian machines for themselves.
